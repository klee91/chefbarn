ABOUT

This is 'The Chef Barn'! (also known as The Coffee Barn) We are a local cafe/restaurant that serves quality coffee along with quality food. We specialize in Korean cuisine, and are also known for our fried chicken. Our pastries are sourced locally from Balthazar Bakery and our coffee is sourced from La Colombe Coffee. We also offer both Delivery & Takeout! Check us out at our location in Edgewater, NJ!

Try our Chicken or Eggs in Hell

Our two most popular items. The fried chicken is made fresh, tender and delicious daily, and the 'Eggs in Hell' are one our recommended specialties.

*We provide both Delivery and Takeout! Call us at (201)-224-2656.
